English Poetry

"Atlantis Unveiled: Navigating the Depths of Love and Loss with Georgia"

"Atlantis Unveiled: Navigating the Depths of Love and Loss with Georgia" is a deeply poignant anthology of poems that artfully intertwines the complex voyage of a romantic relationship. Every poem, with its evocative verses, acts as a gateway to the enchanting origin of love. It captures the captivating emotions that bind two souls together and transports them to a realm where they share dreams and passion. As the collection progresses, it carefully examines the subtle initial indications of the relationship's slow deterioration, effectively depicting the changing dynamics and the unspoken burdens that love endures. The collection reaches a touching climax, bringing us to the profound conclusion of this love story. Here, the author lays bare the abyss of loss and heartache, inviting readers to explore and experience these deep emotions. These poems aim to deeply resonate with individuals who have experienced the complex journey of love. They encourage contemplation on the diverse aspects of human connection, providing comfort and empathy in the midst of undesirable transformations.



Shakespearean Sonnet

Love's Undeniable Bloom
By Medrino Win L. Cruz
'Until I Found You'

In heaven's grasp, we met, old friends turned new,
Yet questions lingered, casting shadows drear,
Could friendship thrive, or love disrupt the view?
A choice to make, a future drawing near.

I'd face the grave before love's flame would wane,
As Juliet to her steadfast Romeo.
To die a noble death, love's sweetest pain,
Than watch our love in friendship's shadow throw.

Lost in shadows deep, Romeo heard my plea,
A solemn oath that echoed through the night,
"I'd never fall unless it's you," said he,
Guided by love's unwavering light.

"I finally found you," whispered fate's decree so grand,
In love's embrace, I've found my promised land.

License to Dream
By Ayzhel Charm P. Gamboa
'Driver's License'

In whispered sobs, heartache's silent moan
I tread these streets, my heart a weighty load
A license in my hand, but not to own
Our love was once a path with tales untold

The signs that marked our love began to wane
A love-struck road now lost in shadows' haze
Though I clung on, your absence cast a bane
And led me through a labyrinth of love's malaise

With every turn, I sought to trace my way
Through memories of you that steadfast persist
But now, my love, you've left, and there you'll stay
In echoes of a love that can't resist

My driver's license narrates a tale of woe
Of love's early signs, signaling to let go

Atlantis of the Heart
By Anekein Caldae H. Garcia
'Atlantis'

A coral castle, carved by love's warm tide,
Now stands in ruin, washed by sorrow's wave.
Our promises, on seagull wings they ride,
Lost whispers clinging to the ocean's grave.

The gentle tides that kissed our love, now still,
In wistful depths, our fates diverged, apart,
Where whispered dreams, in silence, haunt and fill,
The void that shrouds what once embraced the heart.

Our love, a whispered sigh on parchment old,
Sank beneath the waves, where Atlantis sleeps.
A fleeting flame on souls now cracked and cold,
A relic of what was, a phantom that weeps.

Though submerged deep, our memories hold fast,
Lost love, a treasure of the sunken past.


Petrarchan Sonnet

Enchanted: Echoes of Love
By Medrino Win L. Cruz
'Enchanted'

In enchanted realms where melodies play
This is me praying that, on the first page
Not where the storyline ends, in love's cage
My thoughts will echo your name, night and day

Until we meet again, heartfelt prayers light my way
These are the words I held back, in youth's stage
As I was leaving too soon, time's cruel gauge
In life's ballet, enchanted, our hearts in sway

Resist the spell of one's beguiling charm
Nurture your dreams as others' hugs enhance
A plea to fate echoes in this heartfelt psalm
Don't let one linger, heart caught in a fervent dance
Shun narratives that try to inform
In love's song, let our duet gracefully entrance

The Push and Pull, Drowning in Your Flame
By Ayzhel Charm P. Gamboa
'Love the Way You Lie'

In love's beguiling play, you witness my yearn
As passion's flames devour both hearts and minds
Amidst the chaos, love's design entwined
Yet strangely, I'm enthralled, the lessons discerned

Embracing lies, in this pain, we're adjourned
In this dance, the truth's captivity finds
The way we hurt, our destinies aligned
Captivated by the lies I've earned

Love's sweet enchantment starts in the beginning
With fiery sparks igniting souls aflame
Yet, early cracks appear, like art, thinning

Revealing truth, a sadistic game's claim
In the end, we're drowning, torn and pining
Love's contradiction, adoring your name

Tears for a Memory
By Anekein Caldae H. Garcia
'Another Love'

In twilight's cold, where frosty winds declare
My love, in blooms that once adorned the spring
A string of daffodils, no warmth they bring
Just echoes of a time when all was fair

Desire to soothe, to chase away despair
But nights, they haunt, where loneliness takes wing
For tears spent on a love that ceased to sing
Now, love's refrain lost in the wintry air

With fractured hand, the wish to shield from harm
My voice, a weapon, though dulled by winter's sting
A verse, a tender ballad, aching true
But sung to someone else, another's charm
A yearning note, in silence, takes its wing
My tears, they're spent, on love I once knew
